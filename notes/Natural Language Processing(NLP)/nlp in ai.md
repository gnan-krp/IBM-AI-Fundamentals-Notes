### What is NLP?
Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of AI that enables machines to:
- Read text
- Understand speech
- Interpret meaning
- Generate language

---

### Why NLP is Essential for AI
Humans communicate using natural language.  
For AI to interact effectively, it must:
- Understand grammar and structure
- Interpret intent and context
- Respond naturally

Without NLP, AI systems cannot interact with humans meaningfully.
